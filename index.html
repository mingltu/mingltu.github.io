South China Normal University

La Trobe University

I am Postdoctor Fellow at the School of Information Technology in Education, South China Normal University, Guangzhou, China, and a Research Officer at the Department of Mathematics and Statistics, within the School of Engineering and Mathematical Sciences (SEMS), College of Science, Health and Engineering (CSHE), La Trobe Univesity. I obtained my Ph.D.degree in computer science and IT from La Trobe University, Melbourne, Australia, in 2017. Before that, I received the bachelor’s degree in information and computing sciences from Shandong Normal University, Jinan, China, in 2010, and the master’s degree in applied mathematics from China Jiliang University, Hangzhou, China, in 2013.
